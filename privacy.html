Data & Trust

What Information Is Collected

When you use the contact form, the site may collect your name, email address, subject, and message. That information is only used to review your inquiry and reply to you.

How Your Information Is Used

  • To respond to collaboration, freelance, internship, or networking messages.
  • To keep a record of website inquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • To monitor and improve the reliability of the portfolio contact experience.

Data Storage

Contact form submissions may be stored in a database and may also be forwarded to the portfolio owner's email inbox for follow-up. Reasonable effort is taken to keep this information secure, but no internet-based system can guarantee absolute security.

Sharing

Personal information submitted through this site is not sold or rented. It is only used for communication related to your message unless disclosure is required by law.

Your Choice

If you no longer want your submitted message or contact details retained, you may request removal by contacting Ayush directly through the email listed on the website.
